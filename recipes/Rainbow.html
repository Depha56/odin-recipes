Rainbow Pasta Salad II

Pasta salad is a staple for any picnic or outdoor gathering. This is an easy salad to prepare and share with friends and family,
going with just about anything on your menu. Keep it cool with fresh cold cucumbers. I usually add olives to my pasta salad for a salty, briny bite.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (226 grams) tri-color rotini pasta (rainbow pasta)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • cup cucumber, diced
  • cup bell peppers (red, yellow, and green), diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta Boil the colored pasta in salted water according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Prepare the vegetables Wash and chop the cherry tomatoes in half.
  3. Make the dressing In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine the ingredients In a large mixing bowl, add the cooked and cooled pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, red onion, black olives, crumbled feta cheese, chopped basil, and parsley.
  5. Add the dressing Pour the dressing over the pasta salad ingredients.
  6. Optional protein addition If desired, you can add grilled chicken, cooked shrimp, or any other protein of your choice to the pasta salad.
  7. Chill and serve Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or transfer the salad to a sealed container. Place it in the refrigerator to chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
