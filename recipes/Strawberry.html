Strawberry Spinach Salad I

For most of the country, June is when strawberries are in their peak season, and it's time to start incorporating strawberries in all our recipes.
This salad balances sweet and savory. The homemade dressing can be tossed over the salad a few minutes before serving, making it a great make-ahead dish and easy to carry for a potluck gathering.

Ingredients

  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Fresh strawberries
  • Feta cheese (optional)
  • Sliced almonds or walnuts
  • Balsamic vinaigrette dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash the spinach leaves thoroughly under cold water to remove any dirt or debris. Pat them dry using a clean kitchen towel or paper towels.
  2. Hull the strawberries by removing the green stem and leaves. Slice the strawberries into thin slices or halves, depending on your preference.
  3. In a small bowl, prepare the dressing by combining the following ingredients: 3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar, 1 teaspoon of honey, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Whisk the ingredients together until well combined.
  4. Place the spinach leaves in a large salad bowl or serving dish.
  5. Add the sliced strawberries on top of the spinach leaves.
  6. Optional: You can also add other complementary ingredients such as sliced almonds, crumbled feta cheese, or red onion slices to enhance the flavors and textures of the salad.
  7. Drizzle the prepared dressing over the salad. Start with a small amount and add more if desired. Toss the salad gently to coat the ingredients evenly with the dressing.
  8. Serve the Strawberry Spinach Salad immediately and enjoy!
